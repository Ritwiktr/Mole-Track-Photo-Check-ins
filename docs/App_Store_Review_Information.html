1. Account, Paid Content, User-Generated Content & Permissions

1.1 Account registration, login, and account deletion

Not applicable. AI Dermatologist does not require user registration, login, passwords, or accounts. There is no sign-up, sign-in, or account deletion flow. All app data (onboarding answers, habits, photos, settings, and purchase state) is stored locally on the device using on-device preferences. No test credentials are required.

1.2 Accessing paid content or features (purchases / subscriptions)

The app offers a free tier and Premium via Apple In-App Purchase (StoreKit).

Free plan includes:

  • Full onboarding and core app navigation
  • One progress photo upload per rolling 7-day window
  • Limited progress timeline (latest entry visible)
  • Limited home dashboard insights (premium-gated metrics show upgrade prompts)

Premium unlocks:

  • Unlimited progress photo uploads
  • Full progress history / timeline
  • Full home dashboard insights and advanced AI features

How to access Premium (for review):

  1. On the test device: Settings → App Store → Sandbox Account — sign in with a Sandbox Apple ID.
  2. In the app, open Premium via any of:
    • Home → Settings (person icon) → Upgrade to Premium
    • Progress tab → Unlock Premium
    • Tap + to add a second progress photo within 7 days on the free plan (paywall appears)
  3. Select Monthly, Yearly, or Lifetime, then tap Continue.
  4. Complete the Sandbox purchase flow.
  5. Tap Restore purchases on the paywall to verify restore.

1.3 User-generated content, reporting, and blocking

Not applicable. The app does not include social networking, public feeds, user-to-user messaging, or shared UGC platforms. Users may:

  • Enter private text in a one-on-one AI coach chat (stored locally; sent to AI only after explicit consent)
  • Add progress photos stored locally on the device

There is no content reporting, blocking, moderation queue, or public posting. Chat is private between the user and the AI assistant only.

1.4 Prompts for sensitive data or device capabilities

The app requests the following only when the user initiates a related action:

Permission When requested Purpose
Camera User taps + → Take photo Capture dermatology progress photos

Photo Library (add/save) Only if the user chooses to save a photo Optional save to camera roll

Not requested: Location, Contacts, Microphone, Bluetooth, Calendar, App Tracking Transparency (ATT), HealthKit, or push notifications.

AI data sharing consent (separate from OS permissions):

  • After onboarding, users see an “AI features & your data” screen with Agree and enable AI or Not now — use app without AI.
  • Before chat or photo AI analysis, a consent dialog explains what may be sent (onboarding answers, chat messages, selected photos) and that third-party AI providers process it.
  • Users can enable/disable AI data sharing anytime in Settings → AI data sharing.
Internet: Required only for AI features and App Store purchases. Core local tracking works offline without AI.

3. App Purpose, Target Audience, Problem Solved & Value

Purpose:
AI Dermatologist helps users track skin health through photo check-ins, personalized morning/night treatment steps, daily habits, and AI-assisted wellness coaching. The app is for general wellness and education only — it does not provide medical diagnosis, prescription, or treatment.

Target audience:
Adults interested in dermatology and skin health, including users managing concerns such as acne, eczema, rosacea, hyperpigmentation, dryness, or building a consistent treatment routine.

Problem it solves:
Many users struggle to stay consistent with treatment steps, sun protection, and progress documentation, and lack a simple way to visualize skin changes over time. The app consolidates treatment plans, daily habits, and photo check-ins in one experience.

Value provided:

  • Personalized onboarding and home dashboard insights
  • Morning and night treatment planner with check-offs
  • Daily habit tracking for skin-friendly behaviors
  • Progress photo timeline with optional AI observations
  • AI coach chat for dermatology and product consistency questions
  • Optional Premium: unlimited uploads, full history, advanced insights
Disclaimer: For general wellness and education only. Not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Users are encouraged to consult a qualified dermatologist for medical concerns.

Login / credentials: None required. No sample files required — reviewers may use any appropriate photo from the device camera or photo library.

7. Regulated Industry / Protected Content / Authorization

Industry classification: General wellness and dermatology tracking. The app is not a medical device and does not claim regulated medical functionality (no diagnosis, prescription, or clinical treatment).

Authorization:

  • No FDA clearance or medical-device registration — the app does not claim regulated medical functionality.
  • No licensed clinical diagnostic content — coaching and educational guidance only.
  • AI content is generated via OpenRouter using configured models; no redistribution of proprietary clinical databases or copyrighted medical guides.
  • User-submitted photos only; no stock medical imagery requiring separate licensing.
  • In-app and AI system prompts explicitly state the app is not a doctor and users should consult qualified healthcare professionals for medical concerns.

If additional clarification is needed: This app is comparable to lifestyle, habit, and wellness journaling applications with explicit non-medical disclaimers in AI prompts, photo analysis flows, and App Store metadata.
